Meteorology

Weather knowledge is critical for flight safety. This topic covers atmospheric structure, weather systems and interpreting aviation weather products like METAR and TAF.

Exam Pattern

  • Duration: 2 hours
  • Question Type: MCQs (interpretation of charts may appear)
  • Passing Marks: ~70%

Syllabus (Topics)

  • Structure of the atmosphere, pressure & temperature variations
  • Humidity, condensation, clouds & precipitation types
  • Fronts, cyclones, anticyclones, monsoon & large-scale weather
  • METAR/TAF decoding and interpretation
  • Weather charts, surface analyses and upper air charts
  • Aviation hazards: icing, turbulence, thunderstorms, wind shear

Study Resources

  • ICAO meteorology manuals & DGCA guidance
  • Books: Meteorology for Pilots (ICAO/Jeppesen style)
  • Online METAR/TAF decoders & chart reading practice
