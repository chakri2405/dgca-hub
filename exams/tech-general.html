Technical General

The Technical General exam tests a pilot's knowledge of aircraft systems, engines, and general aeronautical engineering concepts.

Exam Pattern

  • Duration: 2 hours
  • Question Type: MCQ & Numerical Problems
  • Passing Marks: 70%

Syllabus

  • Principles of flight and aerodynamics
  • Aircraft structures and materials
  • Piston and jet engine systems
  • Electrical, hydraulic, and fuel systems
  • Aircraft instruments and avionics
