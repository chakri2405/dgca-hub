Technical General

Technical General focuses on aircraft construction, systems, engines and instrumentation — essential knowledge for safe operation and preflight checks.

Exam Pattern

  • Duration: 2 hours
  • Question Type: MCQs (some numerical)
  • Passing Marks: ~70%

Syllabus (Topics)

  • Principles of flight and basic aerodynamics
  • Aircraft structures, materials and loads
  • Powerplants: piston & turbine engine basics
  • Electrical, hydraulic, pneumatic and fuel systems
  • Instruments, avionics and maintenance basics
