Air Regulations

The Air Regulations subject covers the legal & regulatory framework that governs civil aviation in India and internationally (ICAO). It tests knowledge of the Rules of the Air, licensing & DGCA procedures.

Exam Pattern

  • Duration: 2 hours (typical theoretical exam)
  • Question Type: Multiple Choice Questions (MCQ)
  • Passing Marks: ~70% (confirm with official DGCA circulars)

Syllabus (Topics)

  • ICAO Annexes — especially Annex 1, 2, 6 (as relevant)
  • Rules of the Air & separation standards
  • DGCA licensing, certificate types & responsibilities
  • Airworthiness basics and continuing airworthiness
  • AIP (Aeronautical Information Publication) structure and usage
  • NOTAMs, permissions, reporting & incident protocols

Study Resources

  • DGCA official website & circulars (download syllabus PDFs)
  • Jeppesen/Air Regulations textbooks
  • ICAO documents and Annex summaries
